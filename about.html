About ENTEVATE

Innovation, with Intention.

We are a human-centered innovation partner for leaders ready to build what comes next — at the intersection of experiential branding, digital transformation, and strategic roadmapping.

Our Story

A purpose-driven innovation collective.

ENTEVATE was founded on the belief that innovation should be human, purposeful, and accessible. We work at the intersection of three core disciplines — experiential branding, digital transformation, and innovation roadmapping — because we believe the most powerful outcomes happen when strategy, storytelling, and systems thinking come together.

We partner with bold leaders who believe technology should amplify humanity — not replace it. From immersive brand experiences powered by Momentify to co-created innovation roadmaps and applied AI frameworks like AIQUI, everything we build starts with people.

Based in Frisco, Texas, ENTEVATE operates with a distributed team of practitioners who bring deep expertise across enterprise technology, creative production, innovation consulting, and emerging tech platforms.

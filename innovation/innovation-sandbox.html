Innovation Sandbox™

Test What Matters Before You Commit at Scale.

The Innovation Sandbox™ is a contained, facilitated environment for prototyping and validating your most promising ideas — with low risk, fast learning, and a clear signal for what to build next.

The Engagement

Low risk. High signal. Built for the ideas you can't afford to get wrong.

Great innovation fails when resources are committed to the wrong things too early. The Innovation Sandbox™ creates a structured space to test your most promising hypotheses before going all-in — giving your team the evidence to act with confidence.

We design the sandbox environment, facilitate structured experiments with your stakeholders, and deliver a validated signal: what works, what doesn't, and what to do next. The result isn't just a test — it's a confidence-building exercise that aligns teams and sharpens strategy before resources are fully committed.

Inside the Sandbox

Structure meets experimentation.

Every Innovation Sandbox™ is purpose-designed — not generic. Six elements work together to ensure your experiments produce real signals, not just activity.

Hypothesis Framing

Before anything is built or tested, we work with your team to surface and sharpen the core assumptions driving your initiative — so the sandbox tests what actually matters.

Rapid Prototyping

Build minimum viable expressions of your concept — just enough to generate real feedback without committing to a full build. Speed is the point.

Stakeholder Engagement

Get real feedback from real users, partners, and decision-makers — facilitated in structured sessions designed to surface honest reactions, not just positive ones.

Signal Analysis

We synthesize everything gathered during the sandbox into a clear, actionable recommendation — what to build, what to kill, and what to iterate on before committing further.

Full Documentation

Every session, test, and insight is captured and organized — giving you a permanent record of what was learned, who said what, and what it means for your next step.

Team Alignment

The sandbox process builds shared context and buy-in across your team. You leave not just with a signal — but with a group that's aligned and ready to act on it.

How It Works

Four phases. One clear signal.

A structured engagement from sandbox design to validated recommendation — with facilitation and rigor built in at every step.

1
Sandbox Design
Define the scope, goals, environment, and constraints for your sandbox engagement — what we're testing and why it matters.
2
Participant Onboarding
Brief and align all participants on purpose, protocol, and expectations — so every session runs with clarity and psychological safety.
3
Facilitated Sessions
Run structured, time-boxed experiments and prototype reviews with ENTEVATE facilitators ensuring quality, focus, and honest feedback.
4
Signal Debrief
Synthesize all findings into a validated recommendation and next-step roadmap — complete with documentation and a clear decision framework.

Connect Your Venture to the Capital It Deserves.

The Engagement

Funding readiness. Investor access. Capital activation.

Capital exists on the sidelines everywhere — in the networks of angel investors, family offices, and institutional funds that haven't yet found the right opportunity. Sideline Capital Activation is ENTEVATE's structured track for connecting promising ventures to those resources, and ensuring ventures are genuinely ready to receive and deploy capital when it arrives.

Through two complementary programs — the Angel Academy™ and the Innovation Funding Lab® — ENTEVATE equips both investors and founders with the frameworks, networks, and readiness tools they need for a successful, well-structured funding relationship.

The Programs

Two tracks. One mission.

Whether you're an investor looking for curated deal flow or a founder preparing to raise, ENTEVATE has a structured program built for your position in the capital stack.

Angel Academy™

For Angel Investors & Aspiring Angels

A structured education and deal flow program for individuals entering or improving as angel investors. The Angel Academy™ provides curated access to vetted deal flow, a co-investor community, and the frameworks to evaluate, price, and support early-stage ventures with confidence.

Due diligence frameworks & deal evaluation tools
Curated deal flow access from ENTEVATE's venture network
Co-investor community of aligned angel partners
Portfolio management guidance & follow-on strategy

Innovation Funding Lab®

For Ventures Preparing to Raise

A hands-on readiness track for founders preparing for their first or next funding round. The Innovation Funding Lab® walks ventures through pitch development, due diligence preparation, investor matching, and the mechanics of a successful raise — from term sheet to close.

Pitch development & narrative coaching
Due diligence preparation & data room readiness
Curated investor matching & warm introductions
Round mechanics, term sheet review & negotiation guidance
How It Works

From readiness to raise.

A four-phase engagement that takes ventures from funding readiness assessment through curated investor introductions and capital close.

1
Readiness Assessment
Evaluate the venture's funding readiness across pitch quality, diligence materials, deal structure, and investor-market fit.
2
Lab Programming
Work through the Innovation Funding Lab® curriculum with an ENTEVATE advisor — building the pitch, narrative, and diligence package investors expect.
3
Investor Matching
Curated introductions to aligned Angel Academy™ investors and network partners — matched by sector, stage, thesis, and relationship fit.
4
Capital Activation
Navigate term sheets, due diligence, and close with ongoing ENTEVATE support — ensuring the raise is structured for long-term founder success.
Who It's For

Built for founders who are ready — and investors who are looking.

Sideline Capital Activation serves both sides of the capital equation — connecting ready ventures with aligned investors through structured, curated programming.

First-Time Founders

Ecosystem Builders

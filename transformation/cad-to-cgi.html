How We Work

From raw CAD to deployment-ready assets.

A proven three-phase process that takes your engineering data all the way to polished, multi-channel-ready visual content.

Phase 01

Model Prep

We align with your engineering and product teams to acquire, assess, simplify, and finalize CAD inputs. Geometry is cleaned, structured, and optimized for visual conversion — preserving accuracy while enabling photorealistic rendering.

Phase 02

Convert, Model & Render

CAD files are transformed into polished CGI with applied materials, lighting, optimized geometry, and photorealistic rendering. We produce still images, animations, turntables, cutaways, and XR-optimized assets tailored to your use cases.

Phase 03

Deliver & Deploy

Assets are packaged and distributed across your platforms — ensuring consistent, high-quality experiences wherever they're used. We support integration with your DAM, CMS, dealer portals, training platforms, and XR delivery systems.

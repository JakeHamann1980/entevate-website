Proprietary Framework

Is your content ready for the technology of tomorrow?

Most organizations have significant investments in content — product documentation, training materials, marketing assets, technical data — but little visibility into how ready that content is to perform in modern digital and immersive environments.

"Content Readiness is the state of written, visual and animated content and its preparedness for ingestion into human-centered design experiences delivered through mobile, tablet, augmented reality, virtual reality, mixed reality and AI platforms."

The gap between your existing content and what's required to deliver great XR, AI, and digital experiences is your Content Readiness gap. ENTEVATE helps you measure it, close it, and build systems to stay ready.

Three Dimensions

People. Platform. Process.

Content Readiness™ evaluates your organization across three core dimensions. Strength in all three is what enables content to perform at scale.

People Readiness

Are your teams aligned on content goals, roles, and workflows? We assess skills, ownership, and collaboration patterns to surface gaps before they become bottlenecks.

  • Content ownership & accountability
  • Cross-functional team alignment
  • Skills for digital & immersive formats
  • Training & onboarding processes

Platform Readiness

Are your tools and systems configured to support the content strategy you're trying to execute? We evaluate technology fit, integration quality, and scalability for emerging formats.

  • DAM, CMS, LMS integration health
  • Support for 3D, AR/VR, interactive
  • Centralized asset repository
  • Scalability without rework

Process Readiness

Do your workflows enable consistent, high-quality content creation and distribution? We map your processes end-to-end and identify where clarity, accountability, or automation can unlock capacity.

  • Documented, repeatable workflows
  • Metadata, tagging & governance
  • Multi-channel distribution readiness
  • Quality control & audit cycles

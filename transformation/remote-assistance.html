Remote Assistance & Spatial Workflows

Close the gap between your experts and your field.

When a machine goes down, every minute of downtime costs money. When a technician faces an unfamiliar failure, unresolved issues cascade. Remote assistance changes the equation — connecting your best experts to any field worker, anywhere, in real time.

ENTEVATE designs and deploys AR-powered remote assistance workflows from end to end: from use-case definition and UX design to platform selection, deployment, and training. We build spatial workflows that feel natural, scale to your workforce, and capture institutional knowledge along the way.

Whether you're solving for field service, manufacturing quality control, or distributed workforce training — we architect the complete solution.

Platform & Technology

Built on the right tools for your environment.

We're platform-agnostic and hardware-flexible — selecting and configuring the right spatial computing stack for your workforce, budget, and operational environment.

AR Smart Glasses & Headsets

From lightweight industrial safety glasses to full-featured mixed reality headsets — we evaluate and configure the right wearable hardware for your use case, environment, and comfort requirements.

Live Remote Guidance Platforms

Real-time video collaboration platforms with AR annotation, screen sharing, session recording, and expert queuing — built to handle industrial environments, low-bandwidth field conditions, and compliance requirements.

Spatial Content & Work Instructions

Step-by-step spatial work instructions, 3D model overlays, and dynamic procedure guides that anchor to physical equipment and update in real time — reducing error and increasing first-time fix rates.

How We Engage

From concept to field deployment.

A structured four-phase engagement that takes your remote assistance program from use-case definition to fully operational spatial workflow.

Phase 01

Assess & Define

Identify highest-value use cases, assess current workflows, and define success metrics for the remote assistance program.

Phase 02

Design & Architect

Design the full spatial workflow — UX, hardware selection, platform configuration, content structure, and integration with existing systems.

Phase 03

Deploy & Train

Execute deployment with field team training, expert onboarding, and a structured rollout plan that ensures early adoption and real usage.

Phase 04

Measure & Evolve

Track resolution rates, time-to-fix, user satisfaction, and knowledge base growth — using data to continuously improve the program.

Measurable Impact

What remote assistance delivers in the field.

Faster Resolution Times

Expert guidance in real time eliminates waiting for on-site visits — cutting mean-time-to-resolution across your field service operations.

Higher First-Time Fix Rates

Remote experts guide technicians to the right fix on the first visit — reducing repeat calls, return trips, and costly misdiagnosis.

Institutional Knowledge Capture

Every resolved session becomes a reusable knowledge asset — searchable, indexable, and deployable as training content for future technicians.

Reduced Travel & On-Site Costs

Expert knowledge travels digitally, not physically — dramatically reducing the cost per service incident and freeing expert capacity for higher-value work.

Faster Technician Ramp-Up

New field team members reach competency faster with spatial work instructions and on-demand access to expert guidance during real work scenarios.

Scalable Across Distributed Teams

Once deployed, the workflow scales to every region, every field team, and every new hire — without proportional increases in expert headcount.
